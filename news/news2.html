How to Suggest a Game

Help Us Expand Our Game Collection!

Do you know of an amazing game that should be on our site? We'd love to hear about it! Suggesting a game is easy and helps us bring more fun to our community.

How to Suggest a Game

  • Visit the Suggest Page: Click on "Suggest A Game" in the sidebar or navigate to the Suggest page.
  • Fill Out the Form: Provide details about the game, such as its name, description, and where we can find it.
  • Submit Your Suggestion: Hit submit and we'll review it!
  • Stay Tuned: If your suggestion is approved, it might appear on our site soon.

Ready to Suggest?

Head over to the suggestion form and let us know what game you'd like to see!
